## Article Structure

* Include 3 or 4 key takeaways before the introduction

* These should preview the main philosophical insights readers will gain
* Content directly under H1 heading (no H2 subsections)

### Opening Section (H2)

* Create 3 well-structured paragraphs that:
  1. Hook the reader with a philosophical question, paradox, or thought experiment in a conversational way

* Approximately 150 words total
* Use conversational tone throughout - address the reader directly with "you" and "we"

  * Use conversational language - speak directly to the reader with "you," ask rhetorical questions, use "we" to include the reader
  * Include philosophical examples and musical references from jazz, blues, soul, rock, funk, or electronic genres in an accessible, conversational way

  3. End with a thought-provoking question or philosophical implication that invites reader reflection
* Approximately 150 words total

## Transitions
